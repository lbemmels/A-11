Industry Art is a London-based independent agency that represents a select group of photographers and filmmakers who work across fashion, editorial and advertising.

Founded in 2014 by Marco Santucci & Bianca Redgrave, the agency focuses on supporting its artists in the development of their individual practices while also balancing the demands of a successful career within the industry.

Industry Art offers a full production service & has experience producing fashion shoots, digital content & feature films.

Working with strong image-makers, Industry Art is committed to supporting creativity, promoting new ideas and developing productive relationships.
